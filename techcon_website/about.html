History of the TechCon

TechCon was established in 2020 as a platform for technology enthusiasts to connect and share ideas. Over the years, it has grown into a premier event featuring industry leaders, innovative startups, and cutting-edge research.

Each year, TechCon brings together thousands of participants from around the globe to explore the latest trends and advancements in technology.

Our Mission

At TechCon, our mission is to foster innovation and collaboration within the tech community. We aim to provide a platform for knowledge sharing, networking, and professional growth.

We are committed to creating an inclusive environment where diverse voices can be heard and new ideas can flourish.

Past Speakers

Over the years, TechCon has hosted a variety of renowned speakers from different sectors of the technology industry. Some of our past speakers include:

Mark Zuckerberg - CEO of Meta

Mark Zuckerberg, the co-founder and CEO of Meta (formerly Facebook), spoke at TechCon 2022 about the future of social media and virtual reality.

Fred Swaniker - CEO of African Leadership University

Fred Swaniker, a prominent entrepreneur and educator, spoke at TechCon 2021 about the importance of leadership in the tech industry.

Larry Page - Co-founder of Google and former CEO

Larry Page, the co-founder of Google, spoke at TechCon 2020 about the future of artificial intelligence and its impact on society.
